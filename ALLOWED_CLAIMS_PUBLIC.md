# OLERA Public Allowed Claims

## Allowed

OLERA transforms NO-PHI laboratory catalog exports into a catalog readiness queue.

OLERA separates catalog rows into SAFE / REVIEW / HOLD buckets.

OLERA makes duplicate/variant groups, reason codes, effort, priority and next action visible.

OLERA supports LOINC-oriented review with candidate hints only.

## Forbidden

Do not claim automated definitive terminology assignment.

Do not claim clinical validation of LOINC-oriented review outputs.

Do not claim replacement of LIS/LIMS, middleware, laboratory experts or final validation.

Do not claim endorsement by Regenstrief Institute or the LOINC Committee.

## Mandatory sentence

OLERA prepares the review. It does not decide the final LOINC mapping.
